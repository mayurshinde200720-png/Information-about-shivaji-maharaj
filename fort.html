Forts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Major Forts

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj built and captured many forts to protect his kingdom. These forts played a key role in his military strategy and administration.

  • Raigad Fort: Coronation place of Shivaji Maharaj and his capital fort.
  • Pratapgad Fort: Famous for the Battle of Pratapgad against Afzal Khan.
  • Sindhudurg Fort: Coastal fort built to defend against the Portuguese and Siddis.
  • Torna Fort: The first fort captured by Shivaji Maharaj at the age of 16.
  • Rajgad Fort: Former capital of the Maratha Empire and center of administration.
