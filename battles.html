Major Battles

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a brilliant strategist and fought many battles to protect his kingdom and expand the Maratha Empire. Some of the most important battles include:

  • Battle of Pratapgad (1659): Shivaji Maharaj defeated Afzal Khan using clever tactics near Pratapgad Fort.
  • Battle of Sinhagad (1670): Tanaji Malusare bravely recaptured Sinhagad Fort from the Mughals.
  • Battle of Kolhapur: Fought against Bijapur forces to strengthen Maratha territory.
  • Defense against Mughal attacks: Multiple small skirmishes and guerrilla warfare strategies were used successfully.
